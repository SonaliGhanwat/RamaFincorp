Loan For Doctor

Doctor loans are available for professional doctors to help them fulfill their dream to set up their own medical clinic or hospital.You should consider this loan if,

  • 1.You do not have immediate funds available to bear the expenses for setting up a clinic
  • 2.You want to buy new clinical equipments for your medical clinic
  • 3.You want to renovate/improve your clinic

Purpose of loan For Doctor

Some of the basic purposes for which this loan is granted include to set up a clinic or X-Ray Lab, buying new clinical equipments or for expansion, renovation, or improvement of existing clinic and many more. Banks specify the purpose for which they are willing to grant this loan.

Purposes for which loan is granted Purposes for which loan is not granted
Clinic/ hospital construction Personal use
Clinic/hospital renovation Land purchase
Medical equipment purchase Wedding or vacation

Doctor Loan Benefits

  • 1.No requirement of guarantors or collateral
  • 2.Fast turnaround with loan approval in as quick as a few hours
  • 3.Low interest rates compared to rates offered to other self employed professionals
  • 4.End use can include construction of clinic, renovation or purchasing various equipment for medical use
  • 5.Loan tenure can be as long as 5 years
  • 6.Option of fixed and floating interest rate
  • 7.Flexible repayment tenure options available
  • 8.Minimum documentation with approval based on basic KYC, quick background checks, CIBIL score and income proofs

Documents Requried

  • 1.Signed Application Form
  • 2.Education Qualification Certificate
  • 3.Identity Proof (PAN)
  • 4.Residential Address Proof
  • 5.Latest 6 months salary slip for salaried
  • 6.Last 6 months bank statement
  • 7.Last 2 years ITR
  • 8.Copy of Memorandum of Association (MOA) for Private Limited Company or Copy of Partnership Deed for a Partnership Firm
  • 9.Original hypothecated Invoice in case of Medical Equipment Finance Loans
  • 10.Insurance Policy duly endorsed in favour of the bank for the assets given as security to the bank
